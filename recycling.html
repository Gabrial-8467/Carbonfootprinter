Recycling Guide

Why Recycling is Important

Recycling helps reduce waste, conserve natural resources, and protect the environment. It also plays a significant role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions, which contribute to climate change.

Recycling Tips

Here are some tips to get started with recycling:

  • Separate recyclables from non-recyclables in your home.
  • Check local recycling guidelines to know what can be recycled in your area.
  • Use recycling bins for paper, plastic, glass, and metal items.
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle to minimize waste.
